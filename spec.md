### 🎯 Goal
Build a **mini‑golf score‑keeping web app** for a team-building event.

---

### 🧩 Features

1. **Team setup**
   - Pick a **team name**.
   - Enter the **number of people** on the team.
   - Specify the **number of holes** they will play.

2. **Player management**
   - Add each **player’s name**.
   - Allow editing of a player’s **score per hole** (in case of mistakes).

3. **Scoreboards**
   - **Team leaderboard**: show the best team (lowest total score).
   - **Player leaderboard**: show the best individual player (lowest total score).

4. **General requirements**
   - Lower score = better.
   - Editable scores during play.
   - Designed for **mobile phone access** (hosted on free/low‑cost hosting).

5. **Deployment**
   - Host the webapp so teams can use their phone while playing.
   - Prefer **free hosting options**.
